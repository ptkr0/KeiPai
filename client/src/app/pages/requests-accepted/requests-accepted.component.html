How to Submit Your YouTube Video

Your videos are checked every two hours. If you just uploaded your video, please wait a little bit.

If your request wasn't completed, follow these steps:

  1. Make sure you uploaded video after getting your key.
  2. Make sure your video is public.
  3. Make sure you have set correct YouTube tag.

If you completed all of these steps try to refresh your channel in settings page
